Regulatory & Certification Services
Saudi, Gulf & UAE market access
01 / REG
SABER Certification

End-to-end guidance for manufacturers and importers to successfully register products, navigate technical regulations, and secure certificates on the Saudi SABER platform to issue their SABER SCoC and PCoC.

02 / REG
Saudi quality mark (SQM)

Ensure your products meet the required standards with the SASO Saudi Quality Mark (SQM) certification. RACS supports manufacturers and exporters in obtaining SQM certification for safe, compliant, and smooth market access to Saudi Arabia.

03 / REG
G-Mark Certification (Gulf Countries)

Expert conformity verification for low-voltage electrical equipment and children's toys entering the Gulf region, fully ensuring alignment with GSO technical regulations.

04 / REG
UAE / MoIAT Market Access

Navigating the regulatory framework for the United Arab Emirates market, helping your business secure mandatory product registrations and compliance approvals through MoIAT.
